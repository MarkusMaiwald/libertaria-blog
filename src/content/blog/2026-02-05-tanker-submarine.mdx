## The Tyranny of Commons

Vitalik's tanker suffers from what we call **the tyranny of commons**:

> When everyone can use a resource, no one maintains it.

Ethereum blockspace is the commons. Everyone wants to use it. No one wants to pay for its upkeep. The result? **Expensive fees for everyone.**

Every DeFi protocol, every NFT drop, every agent trying to execute â€” all fighting for the same scarce resource. The validators win (they collect fees). The users lose (they pay the costs).

This is not sovereignty. This is **a toll road disguised as a highway.**
