Executive Summary

For an easy, free blog setup on Cloudflare with markdown files, date-based sorting, and hashtag categorization, use Astro on Cloudflare Pages as your primary choice. It offers modern TypeScript tooling, type-safe content collections, and zero-config deployment.

If you prefer maximum speed and ecosystem maturity, Hugo on Cloudflare Pages is excellent. Your preferred stacks (Nimja + HappyX or HTMX + picoCSS + Hmpl.js) are technically interesting but lack documented blog implementations, requiring significant custom development that conflicts with your "easy setup" goal.

For a lightweight custom approach that preserves your stack philosophy, consider Hono + Marked on Cloudflare Workers.
